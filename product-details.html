Super Mario World (SNES) - 1990

Super Mario World, known in Japan as Super Mario World: Super Mario Bros. 4, is a platform game developed and published by Nintendo for the Super Nintendo Entertainment System. The game was a launch title for the SNES and was released in 1990 in Japan, 1991 in North America, and 1992 in Europe.

The story follows Mario and Luigi as they attempt to rescue Princess Peach from the villainous Bowser and his minions, the Koopalings. After arriving in a mysterious land called Dinosaur Land, the brothers befriend a young dinosaur named Yoshi, who joins them on their quest.

Key Features:

  • Introduced Yoshi, who became a staple character in the Mario franchise
  • 96 levels across 7 worlds with multiple exits and secret areas
  • Capable spin jump and new power-ups including the Cape Feather
  • Save feature - revolutionary for its time
  • Iconic soundtrack by Koji Kondo

This copy is in MINT condition and includes everything originally packed with the game: cartridge, box, manual, and all inserts. The cartridge has been tested and saves perfectly.
