Marketplace FAQs

How do I sell my retro games?

Selling on our marketplace is easy! Create a seller account, take clear photos of your games, set your price, and list them. We provide grading guidelines and shipping tips to help you get the best value for your collection.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and cryptocurrency. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption and our buyer/seller protection program.

How does game grading work?

Our grading system follows industry standards: Mint (like new), Excellent (minimal wear), Good (normal wear), Fair (heavy wear). Sellers can also list professionally graded games from WATA, VGA, or CGC.

What are the seller fees?

Listing is completely free! We only charge a small commission (3%) when your item sells. Until 2026, we're running a promotion with 0% fees for all sellers. No hidden costs, ever.

How do I know if a game is authentic?

We have a dedicated authentication team that reviews suspicious listings. Buyers are protected by our authenticity guarantee - if a game is found to be counterfeit, we refund 100% of your purchase.

What's the shipping process?

Sellers handle shipping directly. We provide discounted shipping labels and tracking integration. Most sellers ship within 2 business days, and tracking is automatically updated in your dashboard.
