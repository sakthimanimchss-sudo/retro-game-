ABOUT US

The story behind the #1 retro game marketplace

OUR STORY

Founded in 2010 by two childhood friends with a passion for retro gaming, Retro Market started as a small local shop buying and selling classic games. What began as a hobby quickly grew into the largest online community of retro game collectors and sellers.

We understand the joy of finding that rare cartridge you've been searching for years, or the nostalgia of popping in a game from your childhood. That's why we've built a platform that connects passionate collectors from around the world.

Today, over 50,000 games have found new homes through our marketplace, and we're just getting started. Our mission is simple: preserve gaming history and make it accessible to everyone.
